5. What can you conclude about use of financial services in Tanzania? Which demographic factors are associated with mobile money use?¶

With regards to the age and the type of mobile classification, there is a similar distribution of people who use no mobile money and ones that do, as the ages get higher you findthere is a fewer users. There are more males that use mobile money and atleast another financial service, althoug there are more females who use either mobile money and one financial service seperately, this could mean that they are satified with using one financial service. We also find that a lot of married people will use mobile money and another financial service, this could be that their counterpart uses a different finacial service to their and vice versa. We find that more people who do have land ownership tend to use more financail services than people who do have land. Mobile money is often use by people who do not have land ownership, people who are married, females and people between the age of 20 and 40.
